## 1. Training Program Overview

### 1.1 Legal Requirement

Under EU AI Act Article 4 (AI Literacy), providers and deployers must ensure staff dealing with AI systems have sufficient AI literacy, taking into account:
- Technical knowledge
- Experience and education
- Context of AI system use
- Persons affected by the system

### 1.2 Training Objectives

After completing training, staff must understand:
- What AI is and how MaSubventionPro uses it
- Capabilities and limitations of our AI systems
- How to identify and handle AI errors
- User rights regarding AI-assisted decisions
- When to escalate to human review
- GDPR implications of AI processing

## 2. Training Modules

### Module 1: AI Fundamentals (30 min)
**Target:** All staff

| Topic | Duration | Content |
|-------|----------|---------|
| What is AI? | 10 min | Basic concepts, LLMs, machine learning |
| MaSubventionPro's AI | 10 min | How we use Mistral AI for matching |
| AI vs Human Decisions | 10 min | Advisory role, human oversight |

**Assessment:** 5-question quiz (70% pass rate)

### Module 2: AI Limitations & Risks (45 min)
**Target:** Support, Product, Engineering

| Topic | Duration | Content |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Known Limitations | 15 min | Accuracy gaps, geographic biases |
| Hallucination Risks | 15 min | What can go wrong, examples |
| Error Identification | 15 min | How to spot AI errors |

**Assessment:** Scenario-based test (3 scenarios)

### Module 3: User Support & Escalation (30 min)
**Target:** Support staff

| Topic | Duration | Content |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Explaining AI to Users | 10 min | Clear communication templates |
| Handling Complaints | 10 min | AI-related complaint procedures |
| Escalation Triggers | 10 min | When to escalate to human expert |

**Assessment:** Role-play evaluation

### Module 4: Compliance & Rights (45 min)
**Target:** Management, Legal, Support

| Topic | Duration | Content |
|-------|----------|---------|
| EU AI Act Overview | 15 min | Key requirements for limited-risk AI |
| GDPR & AI | 15 min | Data processing, Article 22 rights |
| User Rights | 15 min | Explanation, opt-out, complaints |

**Assessment:** Written test (80% pass rate)

### Module 5: Technical Deep Dive (60 min)
**Target:** Engineering, Data team

| Topic | Duration | Content |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Model Architecture | 20 min | Mistral AI, API integration |
| Bias Detection | 20 min | Testing methodology, metrics |
| Monitoring & Logging | 20 min | Compliance logging, drift detection |

**Assessment:** Technical review + code walkthrough

## Appendix B: Quick Reference Card

### When to Escalate to Human Review

- User explicitly requests human review
- AI confidence score < 60%
- Subsidy amount > €50,000
- User reports incorrect recommendation
- System displays error or unusual behavior
- Complex eligibility criteria involved

### Key Phrases for User Communication

| Situation | Suggested Response |
|-----------|-------------------|
| User asks "Is this AI?" | "Oui, cette recommandation utilise l'IA pour analyser votre profil. Vous gardez le controle de toutes les decisions." |
| User complains about accuracy | "Je comprends. Nos recommandations sont des suggestions basees sur les criteres publies. Souhaitez-vous qu'un expert humain examine votre dossier?" |
| User asks to opt out | "Bien sur. Vous pouvez demander une analyse manuelle. Cela peut prendre 24-48h. Souhaitez-vous proceder?" |
